About

I hold an M.Sc. in Computer Science from the Universität Bonn, where I specialized in Artificial Intelligence. My research focuses on systematic evaluation of deep neural networks, vision‑language models, and reliable AI for autonomous systems.

Currently, I work with the AI Safety & Certification group at Fraunhofer IAIS, developing methods to detect systematic weaknesses in perception models and leveraging foundation models (e.g. CLIP, SAM, GPT‑4V) for data generation, augmentation, and annotation.
